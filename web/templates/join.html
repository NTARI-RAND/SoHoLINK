Shenandoah Pilot

Contribute Your Device

SoHoLINK pays you to share idle compute capacity from your Windows PC or laptop. When your device is idle, the SoHoLINK agent runs small jobs in the background and you earn from every one. You set the limits. You can stop any time.

What you need
  • ✓ A Windows 10 or 11 PC or laptop (64-bit)
  • ✓ A home internet connection (any provider — Spectrum, AT&T, etc.)
  • ✓ A free SoHoLINK account — takes about two minutes to set up
  • ✓ A bank account or PayPal to receive payouts
How it works
1

Create your account

Sign up at soholink.org. Enter your email and choose a password. No credit card needed.

2

Get your Node Token

From your dashboard, click Get Node Token. Copy the code that appears — you will paste it into the installer in the next step.

3

Run the installer

Download and run SoHoLINK-Setup.msi. When prompted, paste your Node Token. The installer sets up a background service that starts automatically with Windows.

4

Connect your payout account

From your dashboard, follow the Set Up a Node link to connect your bank account or PayPal via Stripe. Payouts are released automatically after each completed job.

5

You’re done

Your dashboard shows your node status and earnings in real time. The agent runs only when your device is idle. You can pause or uninstall any time from Windows Settings.

Common questions

Will this slow down my computer?

The agent only runs jobs when your device is idle. You can set CPU and RAM limits from your dashboard. If you start using your computer, running jobs are paused.

How much will I earn?

Earnings depend on how often your device is online and how much work is available on the network. You keep 60–70% of the compute revenue your node generates. Payouts are processed automatically.

Is my data safe?

The agent runs jobs inside isolated containers — workloads cannot access your personal files, photos, or documents. All communication with the network uses encrypted certificates issued by SPIRE.

How do I stop?

Open Windows Settings → Apps → SoHoLINK and click Uninstall. The service stops immediately and is completely removed. Your earnings are paid out on the normal schedule.
