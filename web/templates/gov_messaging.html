Messaging

Compose one message and send it to operators, members, or both. Delivery uses the same zero-cost net/smtp path as the onboarding email 2FA. Recipients are deduplicated across audiences; the send result reports how many addresses were attempted, how many succeeded, and which (if any) failed.

Audience note

Operators are the coordinator's own counterparties. Members are transitional — Cloudy-owned records still hosted here pending the frontend migration. The member audience (and its recipient list) moves to Cloudy with them.
