Every frontend platform enrolled with this coordinator. From here you can disconnect an operator (revoke its status and hard-revoke its active keys) or revoke it outright. Both are high-blast-radius kill switches — once applied, GetActiveKeyMap returns nothing for the operator and every member it fronts is denied immediately.
