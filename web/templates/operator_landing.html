What SoHoLINK is
Operators

Platforms enroll as operators

A frontend platform (Cloudy, Fruitful) enrolls as an operator, registers a 7-key signing set, proves conformance, and authenticates to SoHoLINK on behalf of its members' machines. Open signup — no invite required.

Coordination

The employment lifecycle

Capability listings, matching and scheduling, the job lifecycle, signed fee declarations, and fiat settlement. SoHoLINK never touches member hardware; its counterparties are frontends and nodes.

Federation · design target

A commons, not a hub

Registry-enrolled coordinator peers with operator-signed sync and witnessed checkpoints. The thin-waist protocol is a dependency leaf, so no coordinator becomes a hub every platform must route through.
