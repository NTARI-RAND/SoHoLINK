Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Who we are

SoHoLINK is a participatory distributed compute platform operated by the Network Theory Applied Research Institute (NTARI), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Our website is soholink.org. Contact: info@ntari.org.

What we collect

From participants:

  • Email address — for account login and platform communication.
  • Password — stored only as a bcrypt hash; we cannot read it.
  • Display name and SOHO network name.
  • Stripe Connect account ID — only if you opt to set up payouts.

From your device when running the SoHoLINK agent:

  • Hostname, country code, and hardware profile (CPU cores, RAM, bandwidth).
  • Heartbeat timestamps and uptime statistics.
  • Status of jobs running on your node and earnings ledger entries.

Automatic:

  • IP addresses — used for login rate limiting; not retained long-term.
  • Session tokens — signed cookies that expire after 15 minutes.

What we do not collect

  • Web tracking cookies or analytics beacons.
  • Browser fingerprints.
  • Files or content on your device outside the SoHoLINK agent's working directory.
  • Personal communications, browser history, or documents.

Why we collect it

  • Email + password: account access.
  • Stripe account ID: routing payouts to your bank.
  • Hardware profile: matching your device to compatible jobs.
  • Heartbeats and metering: calculating earnings accurately.
  • IP address: preventing brute-force login attempts.

Who we share it with

  • Stripe (Stripe Connect) — payment processing. Stripe receives your account ID, email, and earnings amounts. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Cloudflare — serves our website and routes encrypted traffic. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade participant data.

How long we keep it

Data is retained for the period required to operate the platform and to comply with applicable law. Specifically:

  • Account data: while your account is active.
  • Job metering, earnings records, and heartbeat data: retained as needed for platform operation and tax / accounting compliance.
  • Session tokens: 15 minutes, then expire.
  • Rate-limit data: 15 minutes, then cleared.

Your rights

  • Update your account info via the dashboard at any time.
  • Request a data export by emailing info@ntari.org.
  • Delete your account by emailing info@ntari.org. Identifying data is removed; aggregate platform records may be retained as required by law.
  • Opt out of specific workload types (compute, storage, printing) per node via dashboard controls.

The SoHoLINK agent

The agent runs on devices you own. By default it does not enable printing, file storage, or any operation that touches files outside its working directory. You opt in to each capability explicitly via your dashboard. The agent transmits only the metadata listed above to our orchestration servers — never the contents of files on your device, never browser history, never personal documents.

Code signing

The SoHoLINK Windows installer and node agent are digitally signed with an Extended Validation (EV) code-signing certificate issued to Network Theory Applied Research Institute Inc, so Windows displays our verified publisher name during installation.

Changes

Material changes will be posted here and announced to active participants by email.
